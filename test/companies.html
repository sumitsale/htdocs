About Tirupati Balaji Group

"Tirupati Balaji Group", is an accomplished and prominent Corporate for Realty Estate, Builder and Developer in Suburbs Mumbai, Navi Mumbai and Dist. Raigad. Established in the year 2008, the company today is one of the fastest growing venture and catering into various developmental phases of project. A key player in Realty Development Industry TBG is opening its branches into various sectors. The group has been in its core Business of Construction and Property Development since over last 6 years , and has been successfully ventured into different verticals' like Realty & Housing , BPO, to name a few.
