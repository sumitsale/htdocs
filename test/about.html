Tirupati Balaji Motion Pictures Pvt. Ltd

Spearheaded by Mr. Mahendra PawanKumar Singh, Tirupati Balaji Motion Pictures Pvt. Ltd is a Film Production House based at Mumbai, and pioneered in 2014 with a positive initiation and vision to Nurture ideas and bring them into a visual canvas.


We aim to evolve effectual ground breaking unconventional movies and distribute it to the world. We are poised to establish ourselves as an industry leader in producing films that relays and ignites true purpose and passion.


Tirupati Balaji Motion Pictures has branched itself into other domains of production as well and plans for Global Operations, Encompass Motion Picture Production and Distribution, Television shows for Indian Channels, TV Commercials, Event Management.

Tirupati Balaji Motion Pictures Pv.t Ltd. is the vision of Mr.Mahendra Pawan Kumar Singh. He is a renowned name and a leading aspiration in Construction and Property Business in Navi Mumbai, named as Tirupati Balaji Group Pvt ltd.

The group (Tirupati Balaji Group) is into its core Business of Construction and Property Development and is one of the key players in Realty Development Industry. TBG has been working into various diversified sectors, and Motion Pictures Production is it's another new step towards Entertainment.
