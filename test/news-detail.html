Holi Reloaded 2014 INDIA'S PREMIER ELECTRONIC DANCE MUSIC HOLI

" INDIA'S PREMIER ELECTRONIC DANCE MUSIC HOLI " After closing out the 2013 year with a 3,000 person festival in Mumbai, Holi Reloaded "India's Premier Electronic Dance Music Holi " is BACK and ready to transform 2014 .

Providing fans the perfect escape from their everyday lives, Holi Reloaded unites people from all over India and connects them for one magical event. Holi Reloaded allows you to break through and lose yourself in the moment… in the NOW! When we RELOAD we are all one…connected by music, passion, and most of all COLORS.

WHO'S WELCOME? The event will be open to ALL fans of ELECTRONIC MUSIC seeking for a mindblowing EXPERIENCE! Legislation prescribes that you are at least 18 years of age.

THE ORGANIC HOLI RELOADED Our COLORS are skin friendly, non-toxic, approved and certified. Also it easily washes out of most fabrics. We do however recommend not to wear your most prescious clothing. HOLI RELOADED will come to you in more different ways than you will have held possible, purchase your own colors at out paint-stand to bathe in with colour whoever you desire! :)

THE MUSIC The line-up is announced for the event, includes local heroes as well as international djs and HOLI RELOADED residents. Whats sure is that its gonna be a journey through the land of electronic music, so it will include anything from deep sizzling PSY beats to booming dubstep, crunchy electro and energetic house slammers. This is all because choosing for one genre is closing paths to others, and we strongly believe in music as a power to unite.
