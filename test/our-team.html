Our Team

Board Of Directors

Mr. Mahendra Pawankumar Singh - (Managing Director)

Tirupati Balaji Motion Pictures Pvt. Ltd is been professionally run and managed by Mr. Mahendra Pawankumar Singh. He is the Managing Director and Founder of Tirupati Balaji Group.
A Visionary, Enthusiast & Dynamic Entrepreneur, who started his business with one small setup in Mumbai, and successfully expanded his Empire into many verticals within few years.
A strong believer in Humanity, he is a Man with Dreams, and Fire to mould those dreams into a real Souvenir, a real Token of Remembrance.
Serving to Film Industry was one of his dreams, which out of his will and efforts, now turned into reality by the Establishment of "Tirupati Balaji Motion Pictures Pvt .Ltd", his new step towards Motion Pictures and Entertainment.

Mr. Shahnawaz E Khan - (Co-Producer & Board of Directors )

Mr. Shahnawaz E Khan is the Co-Producer and Board of Director of TBMP and currently oversees the entire operations of the company. He is the most important pillar of TBMP.
Basically he is into Construction Business but he has always been allied to Film industry. He has served as Costume Designer for Saira Banu's Production Film "Ab Toh Banja Sajanwa Hamar" in 2006 Starring Ravi Kishan and Nagma. Later on he continued with his Father's Fashion Designing Business for almost 15-20 years, who has been the Dress Designer of renowned actor Mr. Dilip Kumar Sahab for a decade.
His Father Mr. Ibrahim Khan has also produced a Mainstream Hindi Film "Janam Kundli" a 1995 Indian Bollywood film Directed by Mr.Tariq Shah. It stars Jeetendra, Vinod Khanna, Reena Roy and Anu Agarwal in pivotal roles. Now onwards Mr. Shahnawaz will also be serving to the industry by associating with our company.

Mr. Pravarsen K Yesambare - (Co-Producer & Board of Directors )

Mr. Pravarsen K Yesambare has a broad profile that stems from a prolific experience base. A charismatic Choreographer by profession Mr. Pravarsen has over Fifteen years of vast experience in Film and entertainment Industry.
He has been actively involved in Television Dance Shows like "Sa Re Ga Ma Pa" (Zee TV), "Dance Diva's"(MTV), "Junoon" (NDTV Imagine), "Rock n Roll" (Zee TV) etc.
A member of IFDDA (Indian Film Dance Director's Association) and an official choreographer for "Indian Festivals abroad" by ICCR (Indian Council for Cultural Relation's),he successfully accomplished a Broadway Project on International Platform, entitled "Ticket to Bollywood" in 22 countries.
He has done Production Designing for 2010 Commonwealth Games and recently he has been nominated for the member of UNESCO International Dance Council. Being a multi-talented and multi-faceted individual he is also serving into Story Designing, Creative, Direction and Light Designing.
Mr.Pravar is one of the core pillars of TBMP. From the founding of his event firm "Shining Pearlz Events" to the production of Films; his vision has always been to create something innovative and significant.

Mr. Amol Sakharkar - (Co-Producer & Board of Directors )

An Endowed and Proficient Media Personage Mr. Amol possess a vast and valuable experience in the field of Film Production.
He served as the Production Head for popular Daily Soaps such as, "Saarthi" (Starplus), "Tarak Mehta Ka Ulta Chashma" (SAB TV), "Krishnaben Kakhrawala" (Sony), "Arjun" (Starplus) etc.
Besides this, he has also worked for Bollywood Mainstream Cinema and served as Executive Producer for films like "Zor Lagake Haiyya", "Ishqeria" etc. His motive and interest has always been to create content rich cinema, which impelled him to conceptualize and produce Kaw Kaw Kawle", his new initiation towards innovative content cinema.
And now onwards he will be contributing his exquisite experience to our Production by associating with us.
