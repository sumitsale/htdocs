Upcoming Movie

Kaw Kaw Kawle is a story of a village which is a small part of Satara in Maharashtra. It is a drought affected area and the people living here are poor and illiterate. They don't have much sources of earning to run their livelihood. Everything was going on the way it used to be from ages. But one day an incident took place which has changed all dimensions of their personal lives as well as of the village. Which was an announcement made by the Seth of that village. The whole story revolves around that announcement and its multi-dimensional effects on each particular person.
